Welcome to Trident

A Shared Mission, Four Exceptional Schools

We are a family of three Preparatory Schools and one College, all united by a single purpose: to unlock the potential within every child and prepare them to embrace global citizenship in an ever-changing world.

Our journey began with Trident Prep Solwezi, followed by Trident College Solwezi, Trident Prep Kalumbila, and most recently, Trident Prep Lusaka in 2022. Each school shares the same ethos — excellence, care, and community.

Mission & Vision

Nurturing Critical Thinkers and Problem-Solvers

Trident Schools are committed to nurturing critical thinking and problem-solving skills through a meaningful, child-centred education that goes far beyond the classroom.

With teachers from around the world, we foster a supportive and dynamic environment where students thrive both academically and personally. Rooted in strong Christian values, we embrace all faiths and promote respect and understanding within our diverse community.

Our curriculum extends beyond the classroom — offering vibrant sports, culture, service, and environmental stewardship programmes — ensuring a holistic approach to each child's development.

Our History

A Vision Born Over a Decade Ago

Trident Schools' story began in Zambia's Northwestern Province with a vision to provide high-quality education in Solwezi. The initiative was born from conversations between Andrew Whittall and his uncles, Philip and Matt Pascall, driven by a belief that quality education is the most valuable gift to equip children for success.

While First Quantum Minerals (FQM) had already contributed to improving government schools in the region, Trident College was established as a legacy project — a world-class institution that would provide quality education and shape future generations.

The Trident model embraces computer literacy, curiosity, empathy, and a sense of civic responsibility.

2013

Trident Prep Solwezi Founded

The founding campus opens, bringing world-class primary education to Northwestern Province.

2015

Trident College Solwezi Opens

Secondary education arrives, offering Cambridge International qualifications to the region.

2018

Trident Prep Kalumbila

Expansion to the Kalumbila mining community, extending the Trident family to a third campus.

2022

Trident Prep Lusaka

The newest campus opens in the capital, bringing the Trident ethos to a national audience.
